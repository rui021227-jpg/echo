Privacy Policy

ECHO — Last updated April 3, 2026

ECHO is built on a simple principle: your emotional life belongs to you. Your data stays on your device by default. Cloud backup is optional, anonymous, and deletable. We collect the minimum necessary to make the app work — nothing more.

What we collect

Device UUID If you enable cloud backup, a random anonymous identifier is generated for your device. It is not linked to your name, email, Apple ID, or any account. It is used only to associate your backup with your device.

Mood data (optional, premium) When you request a weekly AI reflection, an anonymized payload is sent to our server. It contains only:

// What gets sent — no identity, no precise dates
{
  "day_of_week": "Monday",  // not the calendar date
  "emoji_score": 3,         // 1–5 scale
  "word": "tired",          // your one word
  "breath_taken": true
}

This payload is processed by an AI model to generate your reflection card. It is not stored on our servers after the response is returned.

Subscription status If you subscribe to ECHO Premium, RevenueCat (our payment processor) receives your App Store or Google Play subscription event. ECHO receives only a flag indicating whether your subscription is active — not your payment details.

Push notification token Your notification token is stored locally on your device to schedule daily reminders. It is never transmitted to our servers.

What we do not collect

  • No Name, email address, or any account information
  • No Precise calendar dates (only day-of-week in AI payloads)
  • No Location data
  • No Device advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID)
  • No Analytics or usage tracking SDKs
  • No Camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • No Crash reports linked to identity (Sentry reports are anonymous)

Where your data lives

On your device: All mood entries, reflections, and settings are stored in a local SQLite database. They never leave your device unless you explicitly enable cloud backup.

Cloud backup (optional): If enabled in Settings, your entries and reflections are backed up to Supabase (hosted in the US) using your anonymous device UUID. You can delete your cloud backup at any time from Settings → Backup → Delete Backup.

AI reflection: Processed by OpenAI's API (gpt-4o-mini). Anonymized payloads only. OpenAI's data retention policies apply to API inputs; see openai.com/policies.

RevenueCat & subscriptions

ECHO uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect your App Store or Google Play subscriber ID and subscription event data. ECHO does not receive your payment method or billing address. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

Push notifications

ECHO schedules daily reminders and Sunday reflection prompts entirely on your device using Expo Notifications. Your notification preferences (reminder time) are stored locally. Your push token is never sent to our servers.

Data retention & deletion

  • Local data: Persists until you delete the app.
  • Cloud backup: Persists until you delete it via Settings, or until you delete the app and your device UUID is no longer associated.
  • AI reflection payloads: Not retained after the response is returned.
  • Subscription events: Retained in our database for audit and support purposes. Contact us to request deletion.

Children

ECHO is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
